## Known Gotchas & Bugs We Encountered Today
1. **Empty Database Validations:** The public tenant form (`/[domain]`) threw errors initially because `zod` and our Supabase queries expected valid `university_id` and `kanban_stage` records to exist in the DB.
2. **Row-Level Security (RLS) Blockers:** The default Supabase RLS is very strict. It initially blocked:
   - Anonymous (public) visitors from submitting forms into the `leads` table.
   - Newly signed up users from inserting records into the `profiles` table.
   *We fixed this through the `20260102000000_seed_and_rls_fixes.sql` migration, but keep an eye on RLS as you build more features!*
